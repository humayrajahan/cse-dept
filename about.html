Department of CSE

Computer Science & Engineering department established in 1996. Founder Principal of this department. was Kazi Faruk Ahmed (Treasurer, National University). Founder Chairman was Mr. Pran Krisno Das and Founder Co-Chairman was Md. Nazrul Islam. more than 2000 students (in 21 batches) completed Graduation Degree with good result from this Department. There are 06 (Six) number of permanent teacher in this CSE department. Other guest teachers come from BUET, University of Dhaka and Jahangir Nagar University. CSE department has its own library full of rich books for all students of CSE.
